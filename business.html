4 Simple Steps to Register

1

Reserve Your Name

You need to pick a unique name for your business. Think of 4 options in case your first choice is already taken.

payments Cost: R50.00
2

Register with CIPC

Complete the official registration. You'll need your ID document and proof of address. This can be done online or at a self-service terminal.

badge ID Document
home Proof of Address
3

Tax Registration (SARS)

Every business needs a tax number. Most businesses are automatically registered for tax when they register with CIPC.

4

Open a Business Account

Keep your personal money and business money separate. Many local banks offer free accounts for new small businesses.
