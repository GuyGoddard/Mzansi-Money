Answer 5 quick questions. Get a clear yes or no — with the reason why.

Who generally must file

You must file if you:
• Earn more than R500,000 per year from a single employer
• Have income from more than one employer
• Earn any foreign income
• Earn rental income, capital gains, or investment income
• Received a travel allowance or company car
• Are a provisional taxpayer (freelancer, self-employed, director)
