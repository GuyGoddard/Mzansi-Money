CIPC Registration Checklist

Interactive 7-step business registration tracker for South Africa

Tap each item to mark it done. Your progress is saved automatically.

Progress 0 of 7 done
check
Think of 4 name options

Your name must be unique. Go to bizportal.gov.za to check availability. Names with <4 characters are not allowed.

check
Reserve your business name — R50

Pay R50 at cipc.co.za or bizportal.gov.za. The reservation lasts 6 months.

check
Gather your documents

SA ID (certified copy), proof of residential address (utility bill ≤3 months), and the same for each director.

check
Complete CoR14.1 form (MOI)

The Memorandum of Incorporation can be standard (free download from CIPC website) or customised.

check
Register online and pay R125

Submit everything at bizportal.gov.za. Processing takes 3–10 business days. You'll receive a registration certificate by email.

check
Receive SARS tax number

CIPC automatically notifies SARS. You'll receive a company tax number within 5–10 days of CIPC approval.

check
Open a business bank account

You'll need your CIPC certificate, company tax number, and personal ID. FNB Business Zero and Capitec Business are free to open.

check_circle
Congratulations! 🎉

You've completed the CIPC checklist. Your business is registered and ready to operate.
