Why use Mzansi Money Guide?

Mzansi Money Guide is a free, multilingual financial literacy platform built specifically for South Africans. Whether you need to apply for a SASSA grant, file your SARS tax return, claim UIF after retrenchment, register a business with CIPC, or improve your credit score — we provide clear, step-by-step guidance in English, isiZulu, isiXhosa and Afrikaans. No jargon, no fees, no forms to fill in to access information.
